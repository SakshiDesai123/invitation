गणेश मंत्र

"गजाननं भूतगणादिसेवितं कपित्थजम्बूफलसारभक्षितम्।
उमासुतं शोकविनाशकारकं नमामि विघ्नेश्वरपादपङ्कजम्॥"
Meaning: I bow to the lotus feet of Lord Ganesha, the son of Goddess Uma, who is served by the multitudes of beings, who eats the essence of Kapitta and Jambu fruits, and who destroys all sorrows.
